## Pricing Overview

Nebula API uses a simple pay-as-you-go model. You only pay for the tokens you consume.

## Nebula Models

| Model | Input (per 1M tokens) | Output (per 1M tokens) |
|-------|----------------------|------------------------|
| `nebula-3-pro` | $3.00 | $15.00 |
| `nebula-3-turbo` | $0.50 | $1.50 |
| `nebula-3-vision` | $3.00 | $15.00 |
| `nebula-3-code` | $1.00 | $5.00 |
| `nebula-embed-v2` | $0.10 | — |
| `nebula-diffusion-xl` | $0.04 / image | — |

## Third-Party Models

| Model | Input (per 1M tokens) | Output (per 1M tokens) |
|-------|----------------------|------------------------|
| `gpt-4o` | $5.00 | $15.00 |
| `claude-3.5-sonnet` | $3.00 | $15.00 |

| `llama-3.1-70b` | $0.70 | $0.80 |

<Callout type="tip">
  Third-party model prices are up to **40% lower** than going direct, thanks to our volume agreements.
</Callout>

## Plans

### Free Tier

- $5 in free credits upon sign-up
- 20 requests per minute
- Access to all models
- Community support

### Pro Plan — $29/month

- $50 in included credits
- 200 requests per minute
- Priority access to new models
- Email support

### Enterprise — Custom

- Volume discounts
- Custom rate limits
- Dedicated support
- SLA guarantees
- SSO & audit logs

<Callout type="info">
  All plans include access to every model. The difference is in rate limits, support level, and volume pricing.
</Callout>

## Billing

- Billed monthly based on usage
- Credits never expire
- Automatic top-up available
- Detailed usage dashboard with per-model breakdowns
