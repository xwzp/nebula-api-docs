## Join the Community

Nebula API has a growing community of developers building innovative AI applications. Here's how to get involved:

## Discord

Our primary community hub. Get real-time help, share projects, and connect with other developers.

- **#general** — General discussion and announcements
- **#help** — Technical support and troubleshooting
- **#showcase** — Share what you've built
- **#feedback** — Feature requests and suggestions

<Callout type="tip">
  Our team is active on Discord during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM PST). For urgent issues, use the #help channel.
</Callout>

## Contributing

We welcome contributions! Whether it's fixing a typo in the docs, adding an example, or improving the SDK, every contribution helps.

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch
3. Make your changes
4. Submit a pull request

<Callout type="info">
  Contributors get early access to new features and a special role in our Discord community.
</Callout>
